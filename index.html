Pantry Tracker

Pantry Tracker is a web application designed to help users manage their pantry inventory efficiently. It allows users to track food items, monitor expiration dates, and receive alerts for low stock or expired items. The tool aims to simplify meal planning and reduce food waste by providing a clear overview of pantry contents.
